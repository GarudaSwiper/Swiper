Here's a brief overview of the core symptoms of Generalized Anxiety Disorder:

Excessive worry:

Excessive anxiety and worry (apprehensive expectation) is the core feature. It must be present for more days than not for at least 6 months.
This worry can be about many things, not just one specific event or situation (e.g., work performance, finances, health, etc.).
The person finds it difficult to control this worry.

Accompanying symptoms:

Restlessness or feeling keyed up or on edge
Easily fatigued
Difficulty concentrating or mind going blank
Irritability
Muscle tension
Sleep disturbance (difficulty falling or staying asleep, or restless, unsatisfying sleep)
